Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information necessary to provide the service:

  • Account details: name, email address, and password (stored securely hashed)
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter
  • Basic usage data: login timestamps and session information

We do not collect financial account numbers, bank credentials, or payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your expenses and spending summaries within the app
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • Improve the service through aggregated, anonymised usage statistics

We never sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Cookies

Spendly uses essential cookies only — specifically session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with or send your data to third-party advertising, analytics, or social media services. We may use infrastructure providers (hosting, databases) that process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Export your expense data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of Spendly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app.
